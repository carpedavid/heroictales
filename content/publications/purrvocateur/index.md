You are a special agent who works for the secretive counter-terrorism organization known as the Feline Investigation Service (FIS). Together with your fellow agents, you travel the world stopping dastardly villains from carrying out their diabolical plans.

*SETTING: The secret island lair of the mad doctor, Madame Docteur Catorze.*

*MADAME DOCTEUR CATORZE*
*Hahahahah! I have wired the Explodinators to...*

*SPECIAL AGENT PURRVOCATEUR*
*Explode?*

*CATORZE*
*No, Purrvocateur, to explodinate!*

*PURRVOCATEUR*
*How novel...*

*CATORZE*
*Any old bomb can be made to explode, but an Explodinator explodinates! Really, my arch nemesis, one wonders what kind of a toll it takes on the mind when one has lost as many lives as you.*

*PURRVOCATEUR*
*I’m getting far too old for this.*

*CATORZE*
*It is time for me to take my leave, Purrvocateur. Try not to die! Again…*

*(Purrvocateur’s partner, Special Agent Ecks, enters the scene.)*

*AGENT ECKS*
*Purrvocateur, was that Catorze I just heard?*

*PURRVOCATEUR*
*Of course, Ecks, who else would it be but our arch nemesis?*

*ECKS*
*Fine Purrvocateur, we’ll deal with her later. Now, though, we have to keep the Explodinators from…*

*PURRVOCATEUR*
*Explodinating?*

*ECKS*
*Yes, what else would a device named an Explodinator do?*

*PURRVOCATEUR*
*The possibilities are endless…*

*ECKS*
*Anyway, you keep the device from explodinating while I hack into it to look for clues.*

*PURRVOCATEUR*
*Hack into it? Is this thing connected to the internet?*

*ECKS*
*Sure. How else are you going to monitor it from a safe distance?*

*PURRVOCATEUR*
*Well, in the old days, they used henchmen.*
