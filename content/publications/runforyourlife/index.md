You’re alive. That’s more than ninety percent of your fellow humans got out of this god-forsaken… What do you even call it? Plague? Infestation? Apocalypse? Nobody knows for sure what to call it. I guess everybody smart enough to figure out why dead people started walking around trying to eat living people got killed off trying to figure out why dead people started walking around trying to eat living people.

But you’re here, now. With us, which is the smartest place you can be. There’s safety in numbers. Somebody’s always gotta be awake. Somebody’s always gotta be watching your six. And sometimes, somebody’s gotta act as bait so that the rest of us can get away. Maybe some of us will last long enough that one day the dead will stop moving around and go back to being dead. Maybe…

Or maybe all you can ask for is to be the last to die. Only one way to find out.
