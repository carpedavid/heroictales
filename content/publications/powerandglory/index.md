Greetings, adventurer!

On behalf of the leadership and staff of the Perilous Peregrine Questing Company, allow me to extend a hearty welcome and congratulate you on your decision to affiliate yourself with the finest adventuring companies on Gilea.

Within this package you’ll find useful information on the various goods and services our caring and attentive staff and shopkeepers provide. You’ll also find helpful tips and tricks that you may find useful when navigating the dangerous and often deceptive lands that lay beyond the walls of *Gelbenhügel*.

If you are one of the longer-lived denizens of Gilea, you may remember a time before the War of Ages, when the mighty empires of humans, elves, and dwarves lived lives of peace and prosperity from the shining cities to the bucolic countryside. If so, congratulations on surviving what so many of our fellow citizens did not!

If you were born after the Heretics of Wu opened the Demon Gate and shattered the peace, after pestilence and famine and war decimated the population and devastated the cities and destroyed the empires, you will know only our world today — one dominated by dilapidated ruins, overrun by hungry monsters, terrorized by murderous bandits…

…and filled with endless opportunity for power and glory! So spread your wings, brave adventurer. Explore crumbling catacombs, slay ravenous beasts, and bring back as much of that glittering treasure of ages past as you can fit in your *bag of holding* (always available for the modest sum of 250 gp in our well-stocked and conveniently located *Wunderkammer*).

Oh, and one last thing — in this package you will find an item that is far more valuable than any words on paper can ever be: your Questing Medallion. The work of the extraordinary wizards of Perilous Peregrine, the Questing Medallion is the primary benefit of your membership.

Once you have become attuned to this wonderful device, you can ask it to return you directly to the Welcome Hall of the PPQ Co. house when you have tired of your existing quest. It will also instantly and automatically return you upon death, dismemberment, or permanent disability. So, please make sure you fill out your *Consent for Healing* form before you venture out on your first quest.

May the spirits be ever in your favor,

Andreas Stieler

*Gildenmeister*
