In the US, this weekend is Memorial Day Weekend, when we remember those who sacrificed their lives in service to our country. It also happens to unofficially mark the beginning of summer in most Americans' minds and is often accompanied by spending time with family and friends. Therefore, I thought it would a be a great time to release version 1.1.0 of the [Heroic Tales core rules](/publications/core-rules) -- the first significant update since the initial release.

1.1.0 adds a concrete advancement system, in which the team learns from its mistakes. Practice makes perfect and high-performing teams understand that if they're not winning, they're learning. This release also clarifies the language around calculating the results of the die roll to make gameplay run more smoothly.

Happy gaming, everyone!
