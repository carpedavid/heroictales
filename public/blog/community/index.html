Community Updates

November 12, 2020

I’d love to see the audience using Heroic Tales grow, as I think this can be a vibrant, creative, and supportive community. To encourage the community to expand, I want to offer my assistance in the following areas:

Translations I’ve already had one person volunteer to translate the Core Rules into Spanish. I’d love to see the rules made available in other languages as well. If this is something that interests you, I’d be happy to do the layout in PDF format and host the result on the Heroic Tales website (crediting you as the translator, of course).

Supporting Materials If you use Heroic Tales to power your own game, setting, adventure, or supplement, I’m happy to link to it. If you also make your materials freely available under a Creative Commons license, then I’ll even host them for you and list them alongside the content I create on the Heroic Tales website. I might even volunteer to create a logo for you. I like making logos.

Note: I don’t yet have a way to separate NSFW content from SFW content, so I can’t host that at the moment, and I reserve the right to politely decline to host anything that makes me make a yuck face.
