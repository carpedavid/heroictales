Playtest 4.2.0

November 07, 2020

I’ve updated the Core Rules to Playtest 4.2.0. This seems likely to be the last revision before finalizing the official 1.0 version. I’m including the 4.1.0 release notes as I never released them separately.

Playtest Version 4.2.0

Improved

  • Changed boon #3

Playtest Version 4.1.0

New

  • Changed the name of the system to Heroic Tales from Threat Level.
  • Added an option to cap the amount of momentum a team can lose per roll to make things a little easier for kids and new players.
  • Added boons.

Improved

  • Changed the term threat level to inertia.
  • Based on the feedback from play testing, changed the die rolls that count as a failure to be one and two, neutral to three only, and success as four, five, and six.
  • Changed the definition of complication to be only when a players ends up with more failures than successes on a roll (negative successes). Net neutral results no longer count as a complication because it felt too punitive.
  • Updated inertia tables for the GM.
  • Added guidance that in the event of a team reducing the threat level to zero in the same round that they exhaust their momentum, the team should be considered successful.
  • Modified the role of traits to add one, two, or three dice to a roll, depending on the scope of the trait.
